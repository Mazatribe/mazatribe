What is Mazacoin?
  
  Mazacoin is a digital crypto-currency.  It exists on the block chain (Mazachain) of the miners and full nodes.  The block chain is made up of groups of transactions of Mazacoins.
  
  
How does it work?
  Using wallet software you send Mazacoins from one account to another.
